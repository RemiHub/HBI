Treat yourself to that HBI magic

We are here to magnify the beauty that already exists...

A new decade. A new brand. The same HBI quality. The story of HBI Blowdry Bar began with its visionary, Isha Joseph. The Acronym HBI derives from Hair by Isha,Having almost two decades of professional experience in the Hair & Beauty industry, Isha chose 2020 as the time to pour her knowledge and style into creating a new and exciting brand that provides affordable, bespoke and professional hair services. HBI Blowdry Bar offers premium services including our signature HBI Blowdry, HBI Silk, stylish precision cuts, keratin smoothing treatments,Salon professional treatments to give your hair that healthy luster,bounce, silky shine and love it craves. It’s as simple as that.

We have special offers, loyalty cards, gift cards, professional products for sale and much more. At HBI, we believe in the power of looking and feeling your absolute best. There is nothing better than making a great ‘hair at first sight’ impression. The HBI team are waiting to give you that fabulous HBI look and feeling! Pop in and have fun whilst indulging yourself in all that is HBI.
